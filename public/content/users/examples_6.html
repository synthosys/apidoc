Please note that you can get pi information from rid and nsf_id.

Please also note that sometimes people are on panels even though they are not PIs. NSF assigns reviewers to individual proposals, which are then grouped into a panel to facilitate their review. There may be panelists that are not NSF PIs because NSF utilizes reviewers from many different institutions, including government employees, researchers funded by other government agencies, or researchers who last submitted to NSF over 10 years ago (who are considered inactive within NSF systems).
