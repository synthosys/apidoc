Similar to the first example in the first section, if you are looking for a list of individuals named “Smith” submitting grants, you set the [[object]] as “user” and set the “q” search query equal to your search term (“Smith”) in your [[params]]. Remember to use “page=search” since it is required with “q”.

The output will show a list of individuals with their identifying information and affiliations.
