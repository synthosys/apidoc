Using page=patent[[params]], you can find patents filed for in a particular year (“application_year”) within a certain division (“org”). In this example the [[params]] are org=CHE (chemistry department) and year=2005 (application year 2005). 479 patents and their information, including title, abstract, and grant_year are pulled.
