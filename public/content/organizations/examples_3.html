To find a list of submitted proposals by the same organization as in the 1st example (broken into “decline”, “award” and “proposed”), you add in “page=prop” in the [[params]] field. (You use “&” to add additional parameters.) You use “page” because you are trying to find information specific to the individual organization (by searching his/her personal page). You set page=prop because prop refers to “proposals”. 

In this specific example, the “count” refers to the number of proposals by the organization (219). data for each proposal shows the date of the proposal, the unique identifier (nsf_id), the field of the topic (division) and the title of the proposal.
