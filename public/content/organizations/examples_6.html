Try It! http://nsf.api.smetrics.org/v1/organization?id=0013706000&page=alias

Since organizations may sometimes have multiple unique NSF IDs, one tool to help look for possible duplicates is the alias command. You set page equal to alias in  [[params]] after identifying a particular organization id (in this example, it is 0013706000, University of Colorado at Denver).

The output shows the number of possible duplicates (“count”) and gives identifying and contact information for each different nsf_id. Be careful with the list as some may have similar names but actually be unaffiliated. Also, it may not produce a complete list of possible duplicates as it is quite name-sensitive.
