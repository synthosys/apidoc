Similar to the first example in the first section, if you are looking for a list of proposals with “computing” in their titles, you set the [[object]] as “proposal” and set the “q” search query equal to your search term (“computing”) in your [[params]]. Remember to use “page=search” since it is required with “q”.

The output will show a list of, at most, 25 proposals listed by date with their identifying information and field of research (division).
